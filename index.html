Course Description

This course explores what information literacy means in an era dominated by AI. Students engage in a **Design-Based Research (DBR)** process—understanding learners, exploring AI impact, and testing playful learning experiences. AI is treated as both subject matter and a design tool.

AI Tool Use Policy

Exploration is encouraged, but **transparency is mandatory**. Disclose tool, purpose, and human judgment applied. AI-generated content submitted without disclosure violates academic integrity.

Developmental AI Literacy Stages

Elementary (K-5)

Approach: "Robot or Human?" guessing games to build foundational awareness.

Middle School (6-8)

Approach: "Break the Filter Bubble" Escape Rooms to understand personalization algorithms.

High School (9-12)

Approach: AI Courtroom Simulations focusing on tool advocacy and ethics.
