Course Description

This course explores what information literacy means in an era dominated by AI[cite: 43]. Students engage in a **Design-Based Research (DBR)** process—understanding learners, exploring AI impact, and testing playful learning experiences[cite: 45]. AI is treated as both subject matter and a design tool[cite: 46].

Pillar 1: IL in AI Era

Traditional framework evolution & ethical dimensions[cite: 61, 62].

Pillar 2: Playful Pedagogy

Play theories as a methodology for engagement[cite: 63, 64].

Pillar 3: DBR Process

Evidence-based cycles: Design, test, and iterate[cite: 65, 66].
